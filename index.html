About BRAINS

B.R.A.I.N.S — Breed Recognition & Automated Intelligent Nosological System. A diagnostic support tool for veterinarians and animal health technicians.

📸
Multi-Photo Analysis
Upload up to 3 angles — main, side, and close-up. More photos = higher accuracy.
🩺
Differential Diagnosis
Ranked list of possible conditions with supporting and ruling-out signs.
⚠️
Medication Safety
Automatic PEDIATRIC / GERIATRIC / CONTRAINDICATED classification for all medications.
📈
Future Risk Prediction
Breed-specific genetic risks and condition progression warnings.
☁️
Cloud History
Every diagnosis saved to Firebase — accessible from any device.
🌓
Dark & Light Mode
Switch between themes instantly. Preference saved automatically.
⚠️ Clinical Disclaimer
BRAINS is an AI-assisted pre-screening tool only. All outputs must be reviewed and confirmed by a licensed veterinarian before any treatment is initiated. Not a substitute for professional veterinary care.
